Experienced & Qualified

"As a psychotherapist and counsellor I believe in working collaboratively with clients in order for them to reach a deeper personal understanding of themselves... "

Dr. Tim Burns is an experienced integrative and relational psychotherapist and counsellor.

An accredited member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy and a member of the The British Psychological Society.

With over 15 years experience in Primary Care Mental Health Services for the NHS as well as private practice.

Therapies

"...explore aspects of their past and present issues, find acceptance of aspects of their lived experience that cannot be changed... "

Tim incorporates therapeutic approaches and theories drawn from wider psychological and psychotherapeutic traditions, including Narrative Therapy, Mindfulness, Interpersonal Psychotherapy and Gestalt.
